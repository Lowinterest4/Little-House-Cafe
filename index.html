Our Story

From our tasteful food to our romantic coffee blends, each bite and sip feels special.

Big flavors, warm hearts, and a little house that always feels like yours.

Little House Café began with a simple dream: to create a space that feels just like home. Every cup of coffee is brewed with care, every pastry baked with love, and every dish served with intention.

Our ingredients are sourced locally whenever possible, supporting nearby farmers and artisans. We believe in creating moments, whether it’s a quiet morning with a latte, an afternoon catch-up with friends, or a cozy evening with family.

Over the years, Little House Café has grown into more than just a café — it’s a gathering place for stories, laughter, and memories. We invite everyone to step inside, leave the world behind for a little while, and make themselves at home.
